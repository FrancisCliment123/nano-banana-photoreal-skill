## How to adapt these

1. **Pick a template that's close to your niche.**
2. **Replace the `{age} + clothing + location + action`** to match your creator's
   aesthetic. Keep all the camera / lighting / texture / negative tokens.
3. **Never add text to the prompt** — write your hook and add it in post.
4. **Use 9:16 for vertical feed (Reels, Stories, TikTok)** — never 1:1 for hooks,
   it looks like a passport photo.
5. **Regenerate 3–5 times** — even good prompts have variance. Pick the best.
