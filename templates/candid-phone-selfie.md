# Template: Candid Phone Selfie

**Use when:** the image should read as a random snapshot pulled from someone's
camera roll. Not posed. Not styled. Casual lifestyle.

---

## Template

```
Ordinary iPhone selfie of {SUBJECT with imperfection} in {MUNDANE LOCATION},
{SIMPLE ACTION}, slightly awkward framing with one corner clipped.
{LIGHTING}. Shot on iPhone 15 Pro, front camera, slight motion blur,
slight lens distortion from close range, visible skin pores, cinematic
catchlight in eyes. 9:16 aspect ratio. No text, no captions, no writing.
Avoid: smooth skin filter, beauty retouch, plastic skin, posed, symmetrical
face, airbrushed, Facetune look, AI aesthetic, supermodel look.
```
