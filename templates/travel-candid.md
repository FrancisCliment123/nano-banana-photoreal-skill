# Template: Travel Candid

**Use when:** outdoor / environmental lifestyle. Travel, walking-around,
outdoor activities. Should feel like a friend's phone gallery.

---

## Template

```
Candid photo of {SUBJECT with imperfection} in {SPECIFIC REAL LOCATION},
{NATURAL ACTIVITY}, slight motion blur. {OUTDOOR LIGHTING - specific time}.
Shot on iPhone 15 Pro, rear camera, slight grain, visible skin pores,
peach fuzz, cinematic catchlight in eyes, slight asymmetry in face.
9:16 aspect ratio. No text, no captions, no signs with readable text.
Avoid: smooth skin filter, beauty retouch, plastic skin, airbrushed,
magazine editorial, travel ad feel, influencer aesthetic, AI look.
```
