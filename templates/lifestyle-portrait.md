# Template: Lifestyle Portrait

**Use when:** curated but realistic. Editorial-ish. About Page / press
photo / high-end brand content that should still read as real.

Less candid than the other templates — more intentional framing — but
still anti-slop.

---

## Template

```
Lifestyle portrait of {SUBJECT with imperfection} in {CURATED BUT REAL
LOCATION}, {COMPOSED BUT NATURAL POSE}, {DIRECTIONAL LIGHTING}.
Shot on 85mm f/1.4, shallow depth of field, visible skin pores,
{IMPERFECTION}, cinematic catchlight in eyes, slight asymmetry in face.
Editorial but authentic. No text, no captions.
Avoid: smooth skin filter, beauty retouch, plastic skin, airbrushed,
over-retouched, magazine editorial feel, AI aesthetic, supermodel look.
```
