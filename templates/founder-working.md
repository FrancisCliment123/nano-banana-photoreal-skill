# Template: Founder Working

**Use when:** you need a founder / creator / SaaS-builder portrait that looks
like real work, not a LinkedIn photoshoot. Good for thread hooks, About pages,
founder-story posts.

---

## Template

```
Candid photo of {FOUNDER SUBJECT with imperfection} at {WORK SETTING},
{WORKING ACTION}, slightly tired expression. {DIRECTIONAL LIGHTING}.
Shot on {CAMERA - pick one}, visible skin pores, {IMPERFECTION},
cinematic catchlight in eyes, slight asymmetry in face.
No text, no captions, no writing on screens visible.
Avoid: smooth skin filter, beauty retouch, plastic skin, airbrushed,
LinkedIn headshot polish, corporate portrait feel, stiff posing, AI aesthetic.
```
